Background

Previously, I completed a Master's by Research at IIIT Hyderabad, where I worked with the Cancer Diagnostics (DIAG) lab under Prof. Vinod PK and Prof. C.V. Jawahar. My research focused on deep learning-based automated classification of tumors using histopathology images and collecting large datasets from partner institutions.

Earlier, as a Research Fellow at iHub Data, Hyderabad, I developed a low-parameter model for diagnosing cardiovascular diseases in resource-constrained environments, reducing input requirements to enhance its usability on edge devices.

During my undergraduate studies at Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Delhi, I worked on evolutionary algorithms, COVID-19 prediction models, and environmental monitoring using machine learning during an internship at IIT-BHU.
